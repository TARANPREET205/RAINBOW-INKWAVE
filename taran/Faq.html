FAQ

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, you can either visit our store or use our online booking system. Simply navigate to the "Book Appointment" page, choose your preferred date and time, and fill out the required information.

Do you offer custom tattoo designs?

Yes, we do! Our talented tattoo artists are experienced in creating custom designs tailored to your preferences. Feel free to discuss your ideas with our artists during your consultation.

How should I prepare for my tattoo appointment?

It's essential to prepare your skin before getting a tattoo to ensure the best results. Make sure to stay hydrated, get plenty of rest, and avoid consuming alcohol or blood-thinning medications. Additionally, eat a healthy meal before your appointment to help maintain your energy levels.

Is tattooing safe?

Yes, when performed by a licensed and experienced tattoo artist using sterile equipment, tattooing is generally safe. Our studio adheres to strict health and safety protocols to ensure a clean and hygienic environment for every client.
